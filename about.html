Never Stop Pedaling

I'm 47. I've been riding bicycles since I was a child. I fell out of it for several years then got back into it. I discovered I love it more than I ever imagined!


So, I created this site. The main impetus was that I needed a project for my Code Louisville - Front End Web Development class. Cycling was the only thing I could think of to work with. So, here it is!


I plan for this site to showcase my riding gear and my rides (I take a GoPro on almost every ride. Sometimes, two (one front and one rear)).
